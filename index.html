a bit about

our mission

we are an italian, or more precisely a toscana based dance community group. Each member, or how we like to call ourselves “cinghiali” are dancers, students, full-time workers who love dance but have to come in terms with reality that you can’t yet make a living from it.

the reason why we created this cooperative group to make dancers professionals, creating for them a brand, on which they can build buisnesses to live only from thier passion

let's look at

our offer

Teach

We give from private lesson, to workshop. we have a strategic approach to our method, starting from music theory to avanced skills that you see on youtube

Show

we like to express ourselve, and the best way to do so is through a stage. we mostly do il for fun and for free, if there is no load of work in creating a choreography. On request we can express what you have in mind in pure Art

Branding

This is our principal concern. We believe that every dancer should be financially indipendent, that is why strive to create a brand that suits each dancer. From customize products (wears) to a personal website, to organize shecdules and attract clients

Consulent

We love sharing what we are good at. As said before each cinghiale has thier own specialities. So if you feel stuck in your learning journey and what some help, inspiration or whatsoever, feel free to contact us
